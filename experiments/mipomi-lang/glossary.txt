Hair (general, on head):
Mipomi: DAJOFI
Breakdown: DA (body/flesh) + JO (you/listener - often associated with the head/face) + FI (earth/soil - grows from, or covers like foliage)
Alternative for "strand of hair": DALIKA (DA body + LI fine/detail + KA object)
Hairdo / Hairstyle:
Mipomi: DAJOLO
Breakdown: DAJOFI (hair on head) + LO (shape/form)
Hairy Chest:
Mipomi: DAKOLI (Body-container-detail, chest as a container for organs, with detailed covering)
Breakdown: DA (body/flesh) + KO (place/container - for the chest area) + LI (fine/detail - representing the hair)
Eyebrow:
Mipomi: DAJIKI (Body-ID-tool-covering)
Breakdown: DA (body/flesh) + JI (name/ID - above the identifying eye) + KI (tool/device - like a tool framing the eye, could also be KA object)
Simpler: DAJILA (Body-ID-light, as it frames the 'light' of the eye)
Arm Hair:
Mipomi: DAKALI
Breakdown: DA (body/flesh) + KA (object/stuff - arm is an object-like limb) + LI (fine/detail - the hair)
